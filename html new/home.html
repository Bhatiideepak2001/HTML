Popular Hindu Festivals in India 2024

Hindu religious festivals are celebrated in all states of India, some of which are celebrated at the regional level while others are celebrated at the national level. Since India has a vibrant and diverse mix of states, each with its own language, customs, and festivals, the Hindu festivals themselves can be distinct for each state while some festivals are celebrated across the country. Some examples of such national-level Hindu festivals are Diwali, Ganesh Chaturthi, Navratri, Maha Shivaratri, etc. Some examples of regional Hindu festivals are Vishu, Karva Chauth, Ugadi, etc. These festivals are a great way to bring families, friends, and communities together and honor the ancestral traditions of Hindu culture.
